NIAC2016¶

NIAC2016

The 2016 plenary NIAC meeting and NeXus Code Camp took place in Copenhagen hosted by ESS. As usual the NIAC meeting is being held in conjunction wth and proximity to the NOBUGS conference.

The dates for the Code Camp were 10th, 11th, and 12th October (Mon - Wed). The NIAC meeting ran Thu & Fri (13th and 14th).

Topics for the NIAC meeting were registered as issues in the NIAC repository on GitHub.

Registration and Practical Information was available on the external ESS indico event page.

The 2016 Minutes are availble.
